Help Center.

How do I get WiFi installed?

Submit a request through the WiFi section. Our team will check your location and contact you to schedule installation.

No. You can book installations and site visits without an account. However, purchases require login.

How does M-Pesa payment work?

After checkout, you will receive an STK push on your phone to complete the payment securely.

How long does installation take?

Installation time depends on your location and service availability. Our team will confirm timelines after assessment.
